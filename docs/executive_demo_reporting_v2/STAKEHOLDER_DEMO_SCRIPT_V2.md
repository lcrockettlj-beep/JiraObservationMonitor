# Stakeholder Demo Script v2

## Opening

"JOM is our read-only operational intelligence platform for Atlassian estate visibility. The current build consolidates runtime status, source health, site coverage, discovery, governance, and reports into one operational console."

## Flow

1. Open Command Centre and explain the live estate health score.
2. Show Command Intelligence v2 and explain the risk queue and recommended actions.
3. Open Estate and show monitored vs discovered site coverage.
4. Open Site Workspace from a monitored site.
5. Open Admin and show Governance Depth.
6. Open Executive, Estate and Governance reports.
7. Close with known remaining work and release candidate tag position.

## Closing

"The current release candidate is validated, source-backed, and suitable for controlled stakeholder demonstration. Future work should focus on deployment hardening, trend history, deeper governance analytics, and richer export formats."
